About Us

Welcome to FIXITQUICK, your ultimate destination for all things automotive! Our mission is to empower car enthusiasts and everyday drivers alike with easy-to-follow how-to videos that demystify even the most daunting repairs.

Whether you’re changing your oil, replacing a tire, or tackling complex engine issues, our expert guides provide clear, step-by-step instructions tailored for all skill levels. Alongside our video tutorials, we offer carefully curated tool recommendations, ensuring you have the right gear to tackle every task efficiently and safely.

We’re committed to fostering a supportive community where knowledge is shared, and questions are welcomed, making DIY auto maintenance accessible to everyone. Join us at FIXITQUICK and transform your vehicle maintenance experience into a confident and enjoyable journey!

Although, we are headquartered in Las Cruces, New Mexico, we strive to reach everyone around the U.S. for all car needs.

Our Values

Simplicity and Clarity

FIXITQUICK focuses on simplicity and clarity by delivering concise, straightforward video tutorials that eliminate technical jargon and highlight essential steps. Our clear visuals and step-by-step instructions ensure that users can easily understand and follow along, making automotive repairs less intimidating and more achievable.

Empowerment through Education

At our company, we empower our users by providing accessible, easy-to-follow how-to videos that break down complex automotive tasks into manageable steps. Our educational resources not only build confidence but also equip individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to tackle car repairs independently.

Quality

Our primary goal is to be available and to provide insightful advice to enable our clients to properly conduct at home repairs and maintenance with the upmost quality. We do not accept anything less from ourselves and this is what we deliver to you.

We feel it is extremely important to continually professionally educate ourselves to improve our technical expertise and service to our clients. We welcome you to contact us anytime.

Integrity

At FIXITQUICK, all of our professionals must act with integrity. We believe integrity is the foundation of ethical behavior in our business.
